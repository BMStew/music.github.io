------------------------ Types of Instruments ------------------------

Instruments can be categorized into different groups. There are wind instruments, brass instruments, string instruments, percussive instruments, and more.
Flutes, clarinets, saxophones, and bassoons are all wind instruments. Percussive instruments include drums, mallets, keyboards, and other striking instruments.
Usually, in orchestra, there are a large amount of violins, clarinets, and trombones; more uncommon instruments include oboe, bassoon, and horn.

------------------------ Types of Music ------------------------

  • Classical Music

    • Uses mainly: piano, strings, flute, timpani, etc

  • Impressionist Music

    • Uses mainly: harp, strings, clarinet, piano, etc

  • Jazz Music

    • Uses mainly: saxophone, trumpet, piano, drums, etc

  • Pop Music

    • Uses mainly: bass, voice, guitar, synthesizer, etc

  • Country Music

    • Uses mainly: banjo, guitar, voice, bass, etc

  • Rock Music

    • Uses mainly: drums, electric guitar, electric bass, voice

  • Rhythm and Blues Music

    • Uses mainly: synthesizer, saxophone, trumpet, drums
